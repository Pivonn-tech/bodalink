Branding

Purpose

This document defines the visual and verbal identity of the BODALINK platform to ensure consistency across all applications, documentation, marketing materials, and digital assets.

---

Brand Name

BODALINK

The name represents the connection between passengers, riders, businesses, and communities through modern transportation technology.

---

Mission

To provide reliable, secure, and accessible transportation services through technology that empowers riders and serves passengers.

---

Vision

To become a trusted mobility platform delivering efficient transportation solutions across Africa.

---

Core Values

- Reliability
- Safety
- Transparency
- Innovation
- Professionalism
- Community

---

Logo

The official logo is maintained in the project's design assets.

Logo variations include:

- Primary logo
- Monochrome logo
- Icon mark
- Horizontal layout

The logo should not be modified without approval.

---

Color Palette

Primary, secondary, and accent colors are defined within the design system and should remain consistent across all interfaces.

---

Typography

The platform uses modern, highly legible fonts suitable for web and mobile applications.

---

Voice and Tone

Communication should be:

- Clear
- Professional
- Respectful
- Simple
- Inclusive

Technical language should only be used where appropriate.

---

Brand Assets

Brand assets include:

- Logo
- Icons
- Illustrations
- Application screenshots
- Marketing graphics

Assets are maintained in the project repository.
